About Me

Hello, I'm Noah Koutsoudakis. I am a graphic and motion design student at the University of Antwerp. During my studies here, I've had the chance to learn a lot and use lots of different design software and tools.

I put a lot of thought and intention into everything I create, and nothing is ever random. Everything in my work has a purpose and is part of the final product.

While I explore various aspects of design, I'm especially passionate about animation and branding. These areas allow me to combine storytelling with strong visual identity, which I find incredibly rewarding.
